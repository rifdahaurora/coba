Crème d’Rora

Crème d’Rora was born from a deep love for the gentle art of creating cheesecakes. Each cake we craft is a harmony of the finest ingredients, artisan techniques, and a touch of affection in every step. We believe that cheesecake is more than just a dessert, it’s an expression of taste and emotion, a piece of art that brings joy with every bite. Through refined flavors and graceful presentation, Crème d’Rora is dedicated to delivering warmth, beauty, and delight in every creation.

Inspired by the philosophy “the gentle art of cheesecake,” we offer a variety of flavors that comfort the soul and please the senses. From timeless classics to modern interpretations, every Crème d’Rora cheesecake is made to create a sweet, memorable moment. Crème d’Rora embodies the essence of understated luxury, taste, beauty, and softness come together in perfect harmony.

History

Crème d’Rora was founded in 2020 by Rifdah Aurora, a culinary art enthusiast inspired by the beauty of European desserts, particularly the French-style cheesecake. What began as a home recipe experiment and the excitement of friends who adored its delicate taste soon blossomed into an artisan cheesecake brand dedicated to delivering beauty and flavor in perfect harmony.

The name “Crème d’Rora” carries two meaningful origins:

  • “Crème” represents softness, luxury, and refined taste.
  • “Rora”, derived from the founder’s name, reflects her gentle and artistic character.

    • Crème d’Rora grow as a symbol of understated luxury — elegance that is never excessive, yet deeply felt in every detail of taste and presentation.

    Vision & Mission

    Vision:
    To become a premium artisan cheesecake brand widely recognized for its elegance in taste, beauty in presentation, and dedication to the gentle and refined art of cake-making.

    Mission

    • To create high-quality cheesecakes using the finest natural ingredients through artisan craftsmanship.
    • To combine art, flavor, and aesthetics in every creation to deliver a memorable culinary experience.
    • To embody simple yet elegant luxury for dessert lovers.
    • To continuously innovate in flavor, design, and service while preserving the soft and delicate character of Crème d’Rora.
    • To spread happiness through the beauty and delight of every slice of cheesecake.
